About Me

Enthusiastic and pragmatic information technology engineer with a passion for data and an experience in business analytics with a demonstrated history of working in the field of management consulting. During my experience at ZS I have applied tools like R and SQL to devise targeting strategies and analyze large volumes of data to optimize marketing activities of top pharmaceutical companies. I am currently pursuing my masters in the field of Business Intelligence and Data Analytics from Carnegie Mellon university to enhance my analytical ability and leverage my problem solving and critical thinking skills to derive powerful insights from complex business problems. During my course I took up a corporate project at Numo as a Data Science Consultant where we created an AI powered Chatbot to solve small business lending problems. My learnings at CMU helped me land an Analytics internship at Ernst & Young where we performed predictive analysis using Python, R, Alteryx and PowerBI to help in the financial decision making process.
